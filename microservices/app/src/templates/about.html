About Our Chatbot:



Who is Chad the Chatbot,



24 hours in the making, we present to you our magical chabot!

Our Chad is the epitome of arrogance and conceit, but he is the perfect companion for small talk!

What Did We Use to Make Him?



In order to create Chad the Chatbot, we used...

HTML (to build up our website structure),

CSS (in order to make the website presentable), Flask (to inplement Python into our HTML),

JavaScript (to input Flask and to enable the Python to be tranlated, displaying our output onto our website),

and Deep Learning (in order to "humanize" the computer),




What is it for?



The features of Chad envelopes the concept of "you ask a question or make a comment,

Chad responds!" Thus, you'd spend some of your spare time conversating with our ChadBot!




Why did we make it?



We chose to make Chad for a multitude of reasons.

For one, we found that making a Chat-bot was a learning experience and a chance to try something new,

secondly, we wanted to exercise some of our pre-existing skills,

and finally, we all just love making people laugh!




What can it do for you?



The uses for this program is purely for entertainment purposes.

It's not going to answer life's greatest questions,

however, I assure you it will provide a couple of good, long, and enjoyable laughs whenever

you want something to mess around with!

We hope you enjoy!
